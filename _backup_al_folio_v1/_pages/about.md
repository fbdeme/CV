**Mingyu Jeon** is currently a Research Engineer at **AIO2O** and the Founder of **DE Lab (ModuLabs)**. He received his M.S. in Business IT from **Kookmin University** (GPA 4.11/4.5).

His research interests lie at the intersection of **Large Language Models (LLMs)** and **Graph Machine Learning**, with a focus on applying these technologies to:
- **Medical AI:** Multi-modal analysis using EEG and text (BrainBodyFM).
- **Commerce & Negotiation:** Agentic workflows for automated negotiation systems.
- **Knowledge Graphs:** Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and schema matching.

He has actively published in top-tier conferences, including **NeurIPS** and **CIKM**.
